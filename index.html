About the Doctor

Dr. Vikram Bhullar —
Whole-Person Healing

With over 15 years of clinical experience, Dr. Vikram Bhullar brings a deeply personalised, evidence-based approach to medicine. Trained in both conventional rehabilitation medicine and classical Ayurveda, he bridges the gap between East and West — offering patients a comprehensive path to lasting wellness.

His practice, Elemental Remedies, was founded on a simple belief: true healing happens when we address the body, mind, and spirit together. Every consultation is an opportunity to uncover the root cause of illness — not merely suppress its symptoms.

  • MBBS — Bachelor of Medicine & Surgery
  • MD — Rehabilitation Medicine
  • Diploma in Ayurvedic Medicine & Nutrition
  • Certified Integrative Medicine Practitioner
  • Member, British Society of Lifestyle Medicine
